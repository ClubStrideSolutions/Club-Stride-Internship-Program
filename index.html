Lead Intern Position

                   

Lead Interns (college-level) serve as the frontline youth ambassadors and campaign leaders across all Club Stride sites. They support Core Interns, model digital storytelling, and ensure campaign quality and consistency while driving visibility and impact across schools.

               
                                
                   

Eligibility Criteria

                   
                           
  • Enrolled in a 2- or 4-year college program (1st–3rd year students preferred)
    •                    
               
                                
                   

Required Skills & Experience

                   
                           
  • Demonstrated ability to lead or coordinate projects (campus, community, or class-based)
    •                        
  • Familiarity with digital platforms (Instagram, Canva, Google Drive)
    •                        
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills
    •                        
  • Comfortable with being on camera; must possess an authentic, vibrant personality and willingness to model engagement through video content
    •                        
  • Social media fluency: Instagram (Reels, Lives, Stories) and familiarity with TikTok trends preferred
    •                        
  • Comfortable facilitating small group meetings or peer-based events
    •                        
  • Ability to manage tasks independently and meet deadlines
    •                    
               
                                
                   

Preferred Skills & Experience

                   
                           
  • Experience creating or managing social media content
    •                        
  • Knowledge of public health campaigns
    •                        
  • Prior experience working with youth, community organizing, or civic engagement projects
    •                        
  • Familiarity with basic data tracking or willingness to learn (Google Sheets or Excel)
    •                        
  • Background or coursework in marketing, communications, public health, digital media, or youth development strongly preferred
    •                    
               
                                
                   

Personal Attributes

                   
                           
  • Reliable and responsive communicator
    •                        
  • Strong interest in youth empowerment, public health, or social impact work
    •                        
  • Open to feedback and collaboration with both staff and peers
    •                        
  • Willingness to be a visible representative of Club Stride on campus
    •                    
               
                                
                   

Time Commitment

                   
                           
  • Approximately 100 hours from August 2025 – May 2026 (includes onboarding, content creation, peer engagement, and campaign delivery)
    •                        
  • 6–10 hours per week
    •                        
  • Must be available to attend team orientation and monthly check-ins
    •                        
  • Must have reliable access to internet and mobile device or laptop
    •                    
               
                                
                   

Compensation

                   

$20 per hour (paid stipend)

                   

Total potential earnings: $2,000 for 100 hours

  • Bilingual or multilingual applicants are encouraged to apply
    •

Core Intern Position

                   

Core Interns are high school students who serve as the creative force and peer ambassadors for Club Stride. This internship is for students who are excited to be the face of youth campaigns—creative, confident, and eager to amplify their voice.

               
                                
                   

Eligibility Criteria

                   
                           
  • Must be a current high school student (Grades 10–12 preferred)
    •                        
  • Enrolled at a participating Club Stride campus or invited through Club Stride outreach
    •                        
  • Available for the full internship term (August–December 2025 for Fall cohort)
    •                    
               
                                
                   

Key Attributes We're Looking For

                   
                           
  • Vibrant personality with enthusiasm for representing youth voice
    •                        
  • Comfortable on camera — willing to speak, share stories, or appear in Reels, Lives, and digital content
    •                        
  • Energetic, confident, and expressive, with a desire to influence peers positively
    •                        
  • Open to peer engagement, campus advocacy, and youth-led storytelling
    •                    
               
                                
                   

Required Skills & Interests

                   
                           
  • Interest in storytelling, social media, public health, or civic topics
    •                        
  • Willing to participate in campaign creation and promote healthy messages
    •                        
  • Able to meet deadlines, complete tasks independently, and work on a team
    •                    
               
                                
                   

Preferred Skills & Interests

                   
                           
  • Familiarity with Instagram, TikTok, or Canva
    •                        
  • Experience in school media, leadership clubs, or community events
    •                        
  • Interest in health equity, prevention, and peer advocacy
    •                    
               
                                
                   

Time Commitment

                   
                           
  • Approximately 75 hours (Aug–Dec 2025 for Fall cohort)
    •                        
  • 5–6 hours per week
    •                        
  • Weekly on-campus presence and team meetings
    •                    
                   

Regular Content Contributions

                   
                           
  • 2 Reels per week
    •                        
  • 1 Instagram Live every two weeks
    •                        
  • 1 peer-led event every other month
    •                    
               
                                
                   

Compensation & Support

                   

$17 per hour (paid stipend)

                   

Total potential earnings: $1,275 for 75 hours (Fall cohort)

                   

Additional benefits:

                   
                           
  • Mentorship from Club Stride staff + college Lead Interns
    •                        
  • Access to media training, civic learning, and public health messaging guides
    •                    
               
                                
                   

Bonus Points

                   
                           
  • Students who are natural communicators, enjoy engaging with peers, and thrive in creative spaces
    •                        
  • Priority for youth with lived experience in Solano County
    •                    
               
                                
                   

Important Note

                   

This internship is for students who are excited to be the face of youth campaigns. If you're creative, confident, and eager to amplify your voice, this is for you!
